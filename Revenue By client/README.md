# Revenue By Client Dashboard

## Overview
The **Revenue By Client Dashboard** is a Power BI report designed to analyze and monitor revenue generated by different clients. It helps businesses identify high-value customers, track revenue trends, and support strategic decision-making through interactive visualizations.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze revenue contribution by each client
- Identify top-performing clients
- Track revenue trends over time
- Improve customer and sales analysis
- Support business growth strategies

---

## Dashboard Features
- Client-wise revenue analysis
- Revenue trend visualization
- KPI summary cards
- Interactive filters and slicers
- Dynamic charts and reports
- Drill-down analysis capability

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Power Query
- Data Modeling
- Excel / CSV Data Sources

---

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue
- Revenue by Client
- Top Clients
- Revenue Growth
- Percentage Contribution by Client

4. Explore the dashboard using filters and visuals
