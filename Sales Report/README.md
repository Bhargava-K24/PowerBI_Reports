# Sales Reporting Dashboard

## Overview
The **Sales Reporting Dashboard** is a Power BI report designed to analyze and present business performance metrics through interactive visualizations and reporting insights. The dashboard supports data-driven decision-making by providing a comprehensive view of operational and analytical KPIs.

---

## Objectives
- Monitor key business and operational metrics
- Analyze performance trends and reporting insights
- Support strategic and analytical decision-making
- Provide interactive and user-friendly reporting
- Improve reporting efficiency and visibility

---

## Dashboard Features
- Interactive dashboards and visualizations
- KPI summary cards
- Trend and comparative analysis
- Dynamic filtering and slicers
- Drill-down reporting capabilities
- Performance tracking and insights

---

## Tools & Technologies
